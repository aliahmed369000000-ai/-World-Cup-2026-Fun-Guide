WHAT IS FIFA FAN ID?

🆓

Completely Free

The FIFA Fan ID is a free digital identity card issued by FIFA through the official FIFA app. Any fan worldwide can register at no cost.

🔮

AR Experiences

Unlock Augmented Reality experiences inside stadiums — exclusive behind-the-scenes content, player stats, and more — only accessible with your Fan ID.

🎟

Win Free Tickets

FIFA partners including Verizon, DoorDash and Welcome World Rewards give away thousands of free match tickets — your Fan ID is your entry ticket to these giveaways.

HOW TO GET IT

Takes about 5 minutes
1

Download the FIFA+ App

Download the official "FIFA+" app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). It's free. This is the only official way to get your Fan ID.

2

Create or Log In to Your FIFA Account

Sign up with your email address or log in if you already have a FIFA account. Your account will be linked to your Fan ID permanently.

3

Register for FIFA Fan ID

Inside the FIFA+ app, find the "Fan ID" section and tap "Register." Fill in your name, nationality, and basic information. No documents required for the free version.

4

Get Your Digital Fan ID

Your Fan ID will appear instantly inside the app. You'll also get a unique QR code that you can show at fan activations, partner booths, and stadium entrances.

5

Unlock Exclusive Content

Once you have your Fan ID, go to the exclusive content section in FIFA+ to unlock secret player videos, AR experiences, and promotional content from FIFA's partners.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is the FIFA Fan ID really free?
Yes. The FIFA Fan ID is completely free. You register through the official FIFA+ app at no cost. No credit card required.
Do I need the Fan ID to enter the stadium?
No — for entry you need your match ticket. The Fan ID is separate and gives you access to exclusive content, partner activations, and giveaway programs. However, some fan zones may require it for special events.
Can fans from any country get the Fan ID?
Yes. FIFA Fan ID is available to all fans worldwide regardless of nationality. You just need the FIFA+ app and an internet connection.
What is the AR experience exactly?
Inside stadiums, you point your phone at specific areas and the FIFA+ app overlays digital content — like player statistics, exclusive video clips, and interactive features — on your camera view. It's only available to Fan ID holders.
How do I win free tickets through Verizon?
You need to be a Verizon customer and check the MyVerizon app regularly for ticket drops. Verizon is distributing thousands of tickets across the tournament including Pitchside passes for select matches.
What is "secret content inside the stadium"?
With your Fan ID active in the FIFA+ app while inside the stadium, you can unlock exclusive behind-the-scenes footage, player personal messages, and AR features that nobody outside the stadium can access. This content disappears after match day.
